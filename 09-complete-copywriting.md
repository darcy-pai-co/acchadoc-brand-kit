## SECTION 8: FAQ

### Section Headline
```
Common Questions
```

---

### FAQ 1

**Question:**
```
Is this legal? Will my employer accept the certificate?
```

**Answer:**
```
Yes, completely legal. All certificates are issued by NMC-registered doctors following Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020 issued by the Ministry of Health. These certificates are legally valid and accepted by employers, schools, airlines, and institutions across India.

However, certificates are only issued when medically appropriate. The doctor will conduct a proper consultation and assess whether a certificate is warranted based on your condition.
```

---

### FAQ 2

**Question:**
```
What happens if the doctor can't issue my certificate?
```

**Answer:**
```
If the doctor determines that a medical certificate is not clinically appropriate after the consultation, you receive a full refund of the consultation fee. No questions asked.

This ensures our doctors maintain professional integrity and only issue certificates when medically justified.
```

---

### FAQ 3

**Question:**
```
Can I get a backdated certificate?
```

**Answer:**
```
No. We strictly follow NMC guidelines. Certificates can only be issued for current illnesses or conditions that the doctor can assess during the consultation.

Backdating certificates would be unethical and illegal. Our doctors will not issue certificates for past dates.
```

---

### FAQ 4

**Question:**
```
How quickly will I get a consultation?
```

**Answer:**
```
Doctor typically calls within 30 minutes of booking. The consultation takes 5-10 minutes. If a certificate is appropriate, it's issued and delivered to WhatsApp within 2 hours of your consultation.
```

---

### FAQ 5

**Question:**
```
What if I'm not actually sick? Can I still get a certificate?
```

**Answer:**
```
Our doctors conduct genuine medical consultations. They will ask about your symptoms, health history, and assess whether you genuinely need medical leave.

If you're not genuinely ill or don't meet the criteria for the certificate you're requesting, the doctor cannot ethically issue a certificate. You'll receive a full refund in this case.

We do not facilitate fraudulent sick leave. All certificates must be medically justified.
```

---

### FAQ 6

**Question:**
```
What payment methods do you accept?
```

**Answer:**
```
UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking. Payment is collected after the doctor consultation, and only if a certificate is issued. If no certificate is issued, no payment is taken (or full refund if pre-paid).
```

---

### FAQ 7

**Question:**
```
Is my health information secure?
```

**Answer:**
```
Yes, 100% confidential. We follow strict data protection protocols. Your consultation details, health information, and personal data are encrypted and never shared with third parties.

All our doctors are bound by medical confidentiality obligations.
```

---

### FAQ 8

**Question:**
```
What if I need a fitness certificate but have a health condition?
```

**Answer:**
```
The doctor will assess your specific health condition during the consultation. For fitness certificates, the doctor determines if you meet the fitness requirements for your specific need (employment, travel, sports, etc.).

If you don't meet fitness criteria due to a health condition, the doctor cannot issue a fitness certificate. However, they may be able to provide guidance on next steps.
```

---

### FAQ 9

**Question:**
```
Can I get a certificate for someone else (family member)?
```

**Answer:**
```
No. The doctor must consult directly with the patient. For minors, a parent/guardian must be present during the consultation, but the assessment is of the child.

We cannot issue certificates without directly assessing the patient.
```

---

### FAQ 10

**Question:**
```
How do employers verify my certificate?
```

**Answer:**
```
Each certificate includes:
- Unique verification code
- QR code linking to our verification portal
- Full doctor credentials (name, NMC registration number)
- Our contact details for employer verification

Employers can verify authenticity at acchadoc.in/verify or call us directly.
```
